Obsidian Client is your Minecraft client of choice!

Obsidian Client fits perfectly into Minecraft and offers many features that will improve your multiplayer experience!

Also it's completely free and open source!

Features Download

Features

Some of our best features...

Customizable HUD

Drag HUD components were you want!

Keystrokes

Display specific keys of your keyboard on your screen!

Vanilla Design

Our design is vanilla orientated, so it fits perfectly!

Armor Status

Display your armor with damage percentage on your screen!

Forge Compatible

Obsidian is Forge compatible, so you can add additional Mods as you wish!

General Info Displays

Obsidian has lots of modules that can display you information about the current game on the screen!

...and much more!
